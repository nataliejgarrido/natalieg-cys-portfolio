# Key Takeaways and My Thoughts

I noticed that some, if not most, of the work a SOC L1 Analyst does with alert triaging is based on workbooks — detailed triage and escalation steps for specific types of alerts, such as a suspicious email. I found this to be interesting because I began to think about large companies and what their SOC workbooks look like, and how many workflows they have for certain types of alerts.  

I had the most fun with the SOC Simulator room included with this module. The simulator let me choose between a few different SIEMs, and at first, I chose Sentinel. I ended up having a lot of trouble with it, both with getting Azure set up and with how unfamiliar Sentinel looks to me. I ended up choosing Splunk next, which was much easier to parse.
